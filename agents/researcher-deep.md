   sources). Do not propose from unprocessed raw material — run /capture
   first if needed.

     family `ms_shift` or `swing`, `signal_spec` naming the (not yet
     written) strategy file, risk within guardrail caps.

4. Update `index.md`, append to `log.md`, submit to the orchestrator.

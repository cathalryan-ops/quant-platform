# Displacement

A wide-range candle that closes well beyond a broken level, rather than a
marginal poke through it. Read by practitioners as evidence that larger
participants, not noise, drove the break.

## What matters for us

Displacement is the quality filter for a [[market-structure-shift]]: the
source's testable claim is that daily shifts WITH displacement carry the
multi-day continuation edge, while raw breaks alone fire too often to be
tradeable. Any `ms_shift` signal implementation needs a quantitative
displacement definition (e.g. range vs ATR multiple, close location in
range) — currently an open question.
